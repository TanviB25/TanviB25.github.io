About

We are an international award winning production company. Kabes Media, works with partner clients across sectors in India and around the world to be part of their journeys through telling their stories and the stories that matter to them through films. We have created films for partner organizations across sectors on various topics capturing some heartfelt stories.

Our Services

Films

We make films for organizations with end-end in house production capability. The films are based on the specific ideas and needs of the client partners. We have filmed in 23 states of India and 28 countries across the world!

Research

We conduct qualitative research for our client partners. We have done multi state data collection projects in India and successfully carried data collection with different stakeholders of community and project staff from the field.

Training

We conduct custom designed workshops for organizations and groups on internal communication, film making, film editing, social media management and qualitative research!

Consulting

We do consulting in communications with focus on social media strtagy, social media management, comprehensive review and evaluation of existing communication/social media of your company.
